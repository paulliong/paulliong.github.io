About Me

I am an Indonesian passport holder, was born in Jakarta, the capital city, and have not lived anywhere else. I attended and graduated high school as a member of the Jakarta Intercultural School's class of 2019. Currently, I am a sophomore attending Northwestern University with intent to complete a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science. In terms of focus, I'm still learning but interested in areas of Artificial Intelligence and Game Development.


My hobbies include golfing, playing the saxophone, and gaming. I am also an avid collector of classic stereo systems, CDs, and vinyls; I listen to a broad range of genres, so most of my collection may seem random at times! Growing up in a highly urban area, I wasn't able to experience the outdoors on a daily basis, but I try to go to the island of Bali a few times a year just to experience its beautiful beaches, mountains, and forests.
